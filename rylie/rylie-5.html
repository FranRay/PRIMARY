RYLIE

I… don’t think I should. There are two demons on my left shoulder and no angel on my right telling me what to do.

First of all, there is no way the world’s actually ending. The day that coward they call the US President actually goes through with anything she says, the world will heal instead.

There’s so many things rushing through my head now – and not a single thought I can actually comprehend. Maddox and Primo, I love them with all my heart, but they need to give me time. Like a lot of it. What they did messed me up so bad and it took me years to even begin considering to trust people again.

8 years of just me and my grandma alone in that lonely town. Do they expect to just fix everything after once for a few days?
