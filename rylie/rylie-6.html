“You know… if the apocalypse had a theme song, I think this would be it. It starts off really calm, almost warm – like everything’s normal and nothing’s going to change.” Maddox opens up out of nowhere. We’re sitting on her couch after a long day of stealing our entire neighbourhood’s doormats and hiding them in someone’s dumpster.

She continues, “And then that… bridge? I don’t know. That part where she sings ‘driving out into the sun’ – that part starts and suddenly you’re on edge. Something’s coming and indeed it does come. It ends with helpless screaming and just feels like you’ve gone through an entire world crisis in 5 minutes. .”
