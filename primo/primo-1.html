PRIMO

(FLASHBACK - 2098)

Maddox was the first one I noticed. Pigtails up on her head like two flags of victory. She was screaming at two boys that hogged the slide, demanding them to give her the next turn because she was “older and prettier”.

“Can I have a turn on the slide?“ I yelled out to her.
“Hmm… only if you’re pretty!” she yells back.
“What’s your name?”
“...Maddox. What’s your name?”
“Primo. It’s my dad’s name but he died when I was a baby.”
“Primo… well, that is a pretty name. Okay! You can ride the slide when I’m done.”
