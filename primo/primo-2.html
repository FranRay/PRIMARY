PRIMO

(PRESENT - 2116)

Sometime after leaving, I found a way to start a small tech company and it eventually grew big.

It made my mom happy, I don’t think I ever was. Though, it did feel good to finally pay her back for raising me all on her own. But I can’t shake the thought that says ever since I left, the hole that was once never there started growing bigger than my own body. I knew growing up was hard, but I didn't know it was this hard.

I turn the news on to get my mind off things. It’s been such a busy week at the company and all I want is to catch a moment to breathe.

“BREAKING - the 67th United States President threatens nuclear war on the Republic of Zubrovkia for violating the Cyber Network Treaty that endangered billions of US citizens.

The UN is unclear on whether to take this threat seriously and retreat to a Nuclear Warfare Protocol for countries around the globe.

Currently the US owns over 50,000 nuclear warheads scattered in different territories around the world. If her threat is made true, it might just end the world as we know it. UK Prime Minister Yohan J---”

________________________________________

Great. Even more bullshit. Not even in the comfort of my own home can I escape the crazy things people are capable of puking out their mouths. 50,000 bombs is a big number, World News.

“Do you really think that woman would be dumb enough to push that button and delete the entire planet… tsk.”, I say out loud to no one but myself. My voice echoes and bounces against the walls - even with furniture and a shit ton of weird tech sculptures, I cannot escape loneliness.

I wonder what Rylie and Maddox have to say about this.
